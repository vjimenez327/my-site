ABOUT
After an intensive 3 month journey of building full stack Javascript applications, I am comfortable throughout the stack and love being able to implement new features. I love learning foreign languages and the opportunity to build new friendships with the ability to speak a new language. Software Development does exactly that for me. It's the opportunity to code in a language and build new things either on my own or with a team. I constantly want to learn more and further grow as a developer. It's a different world with its own language that I love to bond over with people.
Outside of code, since I'm new to NYC I like to go out and explore around the city. Whether, it's finding a new restuarant, stumbling across live street music, going to an unexpected event, or just wandering through parks.
I am actively seeking a new full-time opportunity as a software engineer as I continue upon the next journey in my life. Before becoming a software engineer, I worked for Deloitte as a Tech Risk Consultant serving the global software vendors. I also hold a B.S. in Finance from Santa Clara University with a minor in Operations Management & Information Systems.
